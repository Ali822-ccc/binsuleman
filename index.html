FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our products, processes, and partnerships.

What products does BinSuleman export?
We currently export premium Himalayan salt and premium Basmati rice. We are continuously expanding our portfolio with new agricultural products to meet the needs of our global partners.
Which countries do you export to?
We export to 18+ countries across Europe, North America, the Middle East, and Asia. Our key markets include Germany, USA, UK, Canada, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Australia.
What quality certifications do you have?
We are certified under ISO 22000 (Food Safety Management) and HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point). Our products undergo rigorous third-party testing and inspection.
Can I order custom packaging or private label?
Absolutely. We offer full private label and custom packaging services to help you build your own brand. Our team works closely with you to meet your specific requirements.
What is your minimum order quantity?
Our minimum order quantity varies by product. We offer flexible options to accommodate both small-scale and bulk orders. Contact us with your requirements and we will provide a tailored solution.
